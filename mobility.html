About Indel Mobility

Indel Mobility, in partnership with Ashok Leyland, delivers powerful and dependable commercial vehicle solutions designed to support modern transport and logistics operations. As a trusted authorized service centre, Indel Mobility provides certified maintenance, genuine spare parts, and dependable assistance to ensure every vehicle performs at its best. Their expertise in heavy commercial vehicles enables businesses to operate with greater efficiency, reliability, and confidence while minimizing downtime.

Strengthening its commitment to customer support, Indel Mobility continues to invest in advanced infrastructure, including a large service facility capable of handling multiple heavy vehicles daily and offering dedicated spaces for driver comfort. The company is steadily expanding its presence with additional workshops planned across key regions, reflecting a forward-looking approach to commercial vehicle service and long-term mobility solutions.

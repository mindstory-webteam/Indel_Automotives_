About Kerala Volvo

Kerala Volvo was established in 2013 to represent Volvo cars in the state of Kerala. Since then Kerala Volvo has been delivering exemplary service both to potential buyers of the safest car in the world and also to existing Volvo car users. Today. Kerala Volvo boasts of a large database of satisfied customers across the state. Our Technical Team represented India in the Global technical Contest 2022. The purpose built. state of the art Showroom and Service facility in the heart of Kochi and Calicut is built to international standards and stands out as one of the finest facilities in the luxury car segment.

Driven by Volvo’s core values of safety, innovation, and sustainability, Kerala Volvo is committed to building long-term relationships with its customers through transparency, trust, and unmatched after-sales support. With a customer-centric approach, advanced diagnostic capabilities, and factory-trained professionals, Kerala Volvo ensures every interaction reflects the global standards of the Volvo brand. From sales to service, the focus remains on delivering a seamless luxury ownership experience tailored to the evolving needs of discerning customers across Kerala.
